# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in removal from the project or organization at the discretion of the maintainers.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open source community standards.
